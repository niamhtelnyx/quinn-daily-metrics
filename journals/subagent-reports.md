# Sub-Agent Reports

*Logs from spawned sub-agents including supervisor agents and task-specific agents.*

## Template
```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM - [Agent Label]: [Task Description]
**Agent Type:** Supervisor/Task/Analysis/etc
**Duration:** [Actual runtime]
**Success/Failure:** ✅/❌ [Brief outcome]
**Key Actions:** [What was accomplished]
**Learnings:** [Insights discovered]
**Follow-ups:** [Items requiring attention]
**Performance Notes:** [Quality, efficiency observations]
---
```

## Sub-Agent Log

*Sub-agents will populate this file when they complete their tasks.*
